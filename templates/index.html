Find Your Alter Ego

Diversity of thought has been shown to provide substantial benefit in a number of settings. Often achieved through group-decision making, many of the same benefits can be achieved on an individual-level with a multitude of perspectives. Unfortunately, as today’s internet content becomes increasingly personalized, the multitude of perspectives to which one is exposed similarly declines. AlterEgo aims to solve this problem by introducing individuals to their “complement in the room.” Combining years of psychological research, our flagship product presents users with a simple survey designed to assess one’s personality and interest preferences.
